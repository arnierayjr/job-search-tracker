// FAQ

QUESTIONS?
WE GOT YOU.

Is there really a free tier?
Yes — no credit card required. The Free plan gives you up to 10 applications, basic tracking, and 5 Archer messages per month. It's real, not a trial.
What is the Aim Assessment?
The Aim Assessment is Job Tracer's signature AI feature. Archer analyzes your target role, experience, and goals and gives you a visual score — like an archery target — showing where your aim is strong and where to sharpen up. It's the most honest resume gap analysis you'll get, and it's gated to Hunter+.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel any time from your settings. If you cancel, you keep access through the end of your billing period. No questions, no runaround.
What happens to my data if I cancel?
Your data stays in your account on the Free plan. You can export your application history and documents at any time from the Pro plan. We don't delete your account data when you downgrade.
How does annual billing work?
Annual plans are billed once per year and save you 20% compared to monthly. You can switch between monthly and annual in your billing settings at any time.
Is Archer powered by real AI?
Yes. Archer is powered by Claude — built by Anthropic. He's not a chatbot with scripted replies. He reads your actual job history, understands your goals, and gives responses tailored to your specific search.
