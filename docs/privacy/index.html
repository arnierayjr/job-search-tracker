7. Data Storage and Security

Current Storage (Local)

At present, all job application data, contacts, and document content you enter in Job Tracer is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. This means your data lives on your own device and is not transmitted to our servers. The limitation is that your data is tied to the browser and device you use — clearing browser data or switching devices will result in data loss.

Planned Cloud Migration

We plan to migrate data storage to a secure cloud database (Supabase) in the future. When this migration occurs, this policy will be updated, and you will be notified before your data is stored server-side. You will have the option to export or delete your data before the transition.

Account Credentials

Your password is never stored in plain text. It is hashed using SHA-256 with your email as a salt and stored only in your browser's local storage. We cannot retrieve, read, or transmit your password.

Security Measures

We implement reasonable technical and organizational security measures including HTTPS encryption on all web traffic, secure credential hashing, and access controls. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security and are not responsible for unauthorized access resulting from circumstances beyond our reasonable control.

8. Cookies and Local Storage

Local Storage

Job Tracer uses your browser's localStorage and sessionStorage APIs to store your application data, session token, and preferences. This is not the same as cookies — the data stays on your device and is not sent to our servers with each request.

You can clear this data at any time through your browser's developer tools or settings. Doing so will log you out and erase your locally stored job search data.

Session Storage

If you sign in without checking "Remember Me," your login session is stored in sessionStorage, which is automatically cleared when you close your browser tab.

Cookies

Job Tracer may use essential cookies for session management and security. We do not currently use advertising, tracking, or analytics cookies. If we add analytics in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly.

Third-Party Cookies

Google Fonts, which we use to load the Inter typeface, may set cookies in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.
