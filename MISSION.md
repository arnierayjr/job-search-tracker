# Job Tracer — Mission, Vision & Philosophy

---

## Our Mission

To give job seekers a safe landing spot in a daunting job market — a platform where their resumes, cover letters, applications, and contacts tell the full story of who they are, what they've accomplished, and how far they've come.

---

## Our Vision

We believe the job market has failed people.

Most Americans agree — finding work has become an exhausting, dehumanizing process. Job seekers are expected to tailor every resume, craft every cover letter, network relentlessly, and do the absolute most just to get an interview — let alone a job. And after all of that effort, most platforms give them nothing back. No feedback. No encouragement. No reflection of their progress.

Existing job boards don't cater to job seekers. They cater to employers. You can upload a resume, maybe two — and that's it. The human being behind those documents is invisible.

**Job Tracer exists to change that.**

---

## What We Believe

We believe that most people already have what it takes. They just need someone — or something — to help them see it.

We believe that a job search is not just a transaction. It is a journey. And every resume version, every cover letter written, every application submitted, and every contact made is a data point in a larger story — the story of a person growing, persisting, and moving forward.

We believe job seekers deserve to feel human again.

---

## What Job Tracer Is

Job Tracer is not just a tracker. It is a mirror.

It is a platform where your entire job search history lives — organized, versioned, and alive. Where you can look back and see the progress you've made, the skills you've demonstrated, and the effort you've put in. Where nothing gets lost and nothing goes unrecognized.

Job Tracer is the place where people can see that they have made progress — even when the market makes them feel like they haven't.

---

## Who Archer Is

Archer is not just an AI assistant. Archer is a guide.

Powered by Claude, Archer is the companion that sits beside you through your job search — drafting your cover letters in your voice, helping you tailor your resume to each role, tracking your outreach, and reminding you of your own strengths when the process feels overwhelming.

Archer doesn't replace you. Archer reveals you.

The best cover letter isn't the most polished one. It's the one that sounds like you — confident, capable, and real. That's what Archer helps you find.

---

## Who We're Building For

- The person who has been applying for months and feels invisible
- The recent grad who doesn't know how to translate their experience into opportunity
- The career changer who has more skills than they realize
- The person re-entering the workforce after a life event who needs to rebuild their confidence
- Anyone who has ever felt like the job market was built for everyone except them

**We are building for all of them.**

---

## Our Promise

When you use Job Tracer, you will never feel like just another applicant again.

You will see your history. You will see your growth. You will see your story.

And you will remember that you have what it takes.
